Projects

Nonnegative Matrix Factorization - Rice REU STAT-DATASCI 2023

Nonnegative Matrix Factorization (NMF) is a dimensionality reduction technique used in data analysis and machine learning. It aims to decompose a nonnegative matrix into two lower-rank nonnegative matrices. In this project, we implement and compare multiple algorithms for NMF in both synthetic data and practical data. I also find some method to overcome drawbacks of an existing algorithm by some technique in matrix analysis. Potentially, we will apply NMF algorithms in R to analyze Single Cell RNAs Sequence. I am working on this Project with Dr. Eric Chi at the Rice Department of Statistics.

Geodesics Nets Construction

A geodesic net is a network that connects multiple points with the geodesics while ensuring that each additional point is " balanced " and stretched equally by its neighbors through those curves. In this project, we are fining new algorithm that can construct geodesic nets on different surfaces based on the idea of Steiner trees, since Steiner tree are satisfied to be geodesic nets. I am working on this Project with Dr. Ken Richardson at TCU Department of Mathematics .

GO2AI

GO2AI is a project working with AI and the game Go with the goal of optimizing playing. It is inspired from the advent of AlphaGo 2016. I am in the new team to continue working with questions on Go under supervision of Dr. Liran Ma and Dr. Ze-li Dou at TCU Department of Computer Science.
